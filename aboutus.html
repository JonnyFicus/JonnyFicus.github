About Us

Welcome to Fuckus Repairs

Hi, this is fairly straightforward, we are an outcall
service for phone screen repair and replacement.
There are only three questions, the What? Where? and How much?
that need to be answered between you breaking your phone screen and it being repaired.

What do we Repair?

We repair a variety of screen types, but pricing is currently only shown for Apple products.
If you have another type of phone not listed, please contact us for pricing details. In some circumstances,
additional damage may have occurred beyond the screen itself, and may require additional components to complete the repairs.
Costs and time required will vary based on the needs of the individual circumstance.

Where Will the Repair be Completed

When it comes to the "Where" question, that is up to you. We offer you the option of either
driving to us near 7th Street and Loop 101, or we will come to you within a reasonable
distance (sorry, no service to Tucson, etc). If we are coming to you, longer distances may incur a reasonable trip charge.
Usually the trip charge will only be around $5 to $10, for example a trip to Gilbert would cost about $10 for a trip charge.
If you have any questions about the exact additional costs to this, please contact us, providing the details, and we can make a firm pricing available to you.
